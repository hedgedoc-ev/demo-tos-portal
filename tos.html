Demo instance

No production usage, daily deletion

The demo instance is a publicly available instance for trying out HedgeDoc.

You shouldn't rely on the demo instance for production use.
Notes and accounts will be deleted at least daily.

We cannot guarantee that your notes won’t be lost because of hardware or software malfunctions. This is mainly due to the fact that this instance runs the latest unstable code from our repository.

Prohibited content

We may delete inappropriate content without further notice at any time.
The following types of content are forbidden on the demo instance:

  • copyright-protected material and download pages for such material
  • any kind of scam, phishing, malicious downloads, etc.
  • explicit content

Usage of Cloudflare

To make the demo instance available with low latencies in as many countries as possible, we use Cloudflare. One of the downsides is that Cloudflare intercepts all TLS traffic between your browser and the demo instance, which makes the demo unsuitable for private notes.

We generally recommend hosting your own instance for more than testing HedgeDoc’s features.
